Essay 2 Writing Tips

ENGL 100

Niko Kompella

 

  • Example thesis statement: Film is a negative because it embraces stereotypes of ___. The film tries to be positive but ultimately embraces negative stereotypes of _____.
    • The film is a net positive in its reflection of stereotypes of ____.
  • Read “against the grain” - don’t talk about obvious stereotypes in the film or TV show
