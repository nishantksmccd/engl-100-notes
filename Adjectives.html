·         Adjective – describes or modifies a noun

·         No limit to the number of adjectives that describe a noun

·         Coordinate vs. Cumulative Adjectives

o   Coordinate adjectives modify the noun separately

§  They pass the “and test”

§  They pass the swap test

§  “Dr. Fitzgerald is a handsome (and) fascinating man”

§  “Dr. Fitzgerald is a fascinating, handsome man”

o   Cumulative adjectives work together to modify the noun
