Essay 1 Writing Tips

ENGL 100

Niko Kompella

 

  • Multi-vibe strategy: each vibe in the thesis statement is its own paragraph
    • Each paragraph has multiple pieces of evidence supporting the claim about the vibe
  • Keep the information relevant to your topic sentence
  • Aspect approach: one vibe, with multiple aspects to the vibe
    • Intro paragraph: one and a half vibes
    • Body paragraph: aspect of the store producing the vibe
    • Evidence used for each aspect must contribute to the vibe we choose
      • if it’s a hyphenated vibe (casual-healthy) evidence only needs to prove one
  • Add room for elaboration
    • “The lights are bright” vs. “Through its elaborate lighting scheme, the Gap ____”
  • “The store uses its decor and design to create a ____ atmosphere” - basic thesis format
    • “One way the store creates attracts customers is through _______.” - basic topic sentence for multi-vibe
    • “Another way the store creates (vibe) is through _____” - good topic sentence for aspect approach
