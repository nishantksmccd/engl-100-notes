1. Use a comma with FANBOYS to link two independent clauses

2. Use a comma after an introduction leading to an independent clause

3. Between all items on a list of three or more

4. When two coordinate adjectives modify a noun

5. Around interruptions and “by the way” information, but not around necessary and identifying information

6. To offset a contrast with “but”

7. Around years if an exact date is given.

8. Around country and state names if the city is named.
